About Me

Welcome to my personal website, made with Vanilla HTML and CSS. I'm Nik Zral, a first-year student at the University of Victoria, from beautiful North Vancouver, BC. Beyond academia, I find my passion in coaching competitive sailing and indulging in freestyle skiing. With a programming journey spanning six years in HTML, CSS, and JS, four years in Python, and two years in Swift and SwiftUI, I've also delved into the realms of Node.js, PHP, and Pinescript. This website serves as a portfolio, showcasing some of my notable projects and reflecting my enthusiasm for the world of coding, economics and mathematics. Explore and enjoy!

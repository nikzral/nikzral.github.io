About Me

Welcome to my personal website! I'm Nik Zral, a first-year student at the University of Victoria, from beautiful North Vancouver, BC. Beyond academia, I find my passion in coaching competitive sailing and indulging in freestyle skiing.

With a programming journey spanning six years in HTML, CSS, and JS, four years in Python, and two years in Swift and SwiftUI, I've also delved into the realms of Node.js, PHP, and Pinescript. My current focus is on the exciting world of FinTech, where I'm exploring the intersection of finance, technology, and artificial intelligence.
